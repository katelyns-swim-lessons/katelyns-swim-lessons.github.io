Frequently Asked Questions Continued

What do you recommend in terms of floating/swimming devices?

Puddle jumpers are okay to use until your child takes lessons. After your child takes lessons I recommend a speedo swim vest. This will allow your child to use big arms and practice their skills, while also being in a floating device. I recommend families to have their child practice their swimming before they can "earn" their vest. This helps kids to remember what they have learned and progress even further. Please do not bring a floating device to swim lessons

speedo swim vest.

Can we bring food?

There is no food allowed in the pool area. Due to allergies of family members and students, I have a strict no food policy in the pool and on the deck. Water is allowed

Can I bring siblings?

Yes, you may bring siblings. Remember, you are entering someone else's yard and property so please be respectful. I suggest that you bring something quiet to entertain your children such as electronics or a coloring book while your children sit next to you on the pool deck

What happens if it rains?

I teach in the rain, if it is thundering or lightning, lessons will be cancelled. I will do my best to reschedule lessons. If possible, group lessons will be rescheduled for Thursday and private lessons will be made up throughtout the week. I will post to Facebook and call each family if class is cancelled.

What happens if I have to cancel, will I get my money back?

If you have to cancel for group lessons before they start you will not get your $10 deposit back. If you cancel in the middle of the week you will not be refunded. Any missed sessions will be rescheduled if possible.
