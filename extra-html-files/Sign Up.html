If you are unsure of your child’s level, we can discuss that during registration as well. Levels: 0: Students have no experience in swimming lessons and little to no experience in the water. 0+: Students have some experience in swimming lessons but are not swimming on their own. OR Students have no experience in swimming lessons but are extremely comfortable in the water. 1: Students can swim with their faces in the water on their own, but not confidently and not long distances. 1+: Students are more comfortable and confident in the water. Students will learn how to jump off the diving board and swim to the ladder. 2: Students can swim across the width of the pool. The students are starting to show interest in taking breaths on their own. 3: Students can take breaths on their own. During this level, students will become comfortable floating on their backs. 3+: Students are starting to swim the length of the pool but might get tired and need some assistance. 4: Students can comfortably swim the length of the pool and are starting to learn new strokes and dives.
